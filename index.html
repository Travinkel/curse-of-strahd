A Summons to the Mists of Barovia

Dearest Esteemed Adventurer,

In the distant, shadow-cloaked land of Barovia, veiled by the mists of ancient secrets and the echoes of the damned, a call has been issued from the depths of Castle Ravenloft. Strahd von Zarovich, the timeless lord of this cursed realm, extends an invitation—one not easily refused.

You, brave soul, have been chosen. By what fate or ill fortune, one can scarcely say, yet the path before you is irrevocably laid. A carriage drawn by shadowed steeds awaits to carry you through the fog-swathed valleys and moorlands that few have tread and even fewer have escaped.

Within the crumbling walls of Ravenloft, corridors wind and secrets beckon. The air is thick with the perfume of decayed grandeur and the silent whispers of those who walked before you, their souls ensnared in Strahd’s unyielding grasp. Here, in the heart of darkness, your mettle shall be tested against the backdrop of eternal night.

Prepare, then, to embark upon a journey where the boundaries between the living and the spectral dissolve. Gather your courage, your steel, and your wits. The master of Ravenloft awaits your arrival with impatience, his ancient eyes burning with a light that is both invitation and challenge.

May your heart be stalwart and your resolve unwavering, for once the mists envelop you, the world you know will be but a distant memory. This is the adventure of a lifetime—an invitation to change fate itself.

Do you dare answer the call?

Yours in the shadow of the spire,

Strahd von Zarovich
